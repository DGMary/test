What is a real-life room escape game?

Today a company of 2-5 friends should not be considering for long what to do in their free time anymore, in case all the new released films have been watched and no one is hungry. A new type of entertainment - real escape games - has emerged in Japan and China in 2007 and quickly spread to the USA and Europe. That is an adventure game in which you are locked with your friends and have to solve a series of puzzles and riddles in order to escape the room within a particular time limit. This game partly reminds of a scavenger hunt but inside the building, partly of computer game. The intellectual part of this entertainment is attracting more and more people all over the world. What else is so enticing about escape rooms?

The task is to get out the room, which is decorated according to a particular theme, in one hour. Mobile phones, tablets - all of them has to remain outside the room, so you can devote all your time to hunting for clues and solving puzzles. The only methods you are allowed to apply is your quickness of wit, smartness, and logic. Nowadays it feels extraordinary to stay away from your mobile device for an hour. If on top of that you become a hero of an incredible adventure - be sure to absolutely enjoy this game.

If you have decided to escape the room, be prepared for a dense variety offered by different companies in your city. You may find yourself in an escape room hint hunting and solving logic tasks. In contrast, you might end up in a real-life quest where you will also enjoy an intriguing plot as in a good detective story.
